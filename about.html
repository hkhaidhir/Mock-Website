About Us

Company Profile

UOA Group has built excellence for many years since its inception in 1987. Over the years, our group has
grown from strength to strength and becoming one of the leading property developers in Malaysia. Our
business has presence in Malaysia, Australia, Singapore and Vietnam.

In 2021, UOA Development has been conferred the No 1 spot for the Top Property Developers Awards
(TPDA), by The Edge Property Award. We have built excellence and has huge pools of Talents in various
industries and segmentation such as construction, property development, hospitality, medical &
healthcare, car park management, facilities management, REITs, and more.

UOA Academy is the corporate training arm of UOA Group, playing a leading role in meeting the ever-
changing needs of business organizations. We provide a wide range of well-designed programs, specially
catered for business organizations with different training needs.

UOA Academy is a Leadership Centre to develop Leaders under our 4 Pillars of Greatness; Management
& Leadership, Stakeholder Relations, Human Capital Management, Digital Transformation & Innovation.
As a corporate training arm for UOA Group, UOA Academy play a leading role in meeting the ever-
changing needs of individual & business trends. We provide a wide range of well-designed programs,
specially catered for individual & business growth, with our future-proof approach and delivery.
